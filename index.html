Welcome to Our Assembly

Beloved, it is a joy to have you here. Jesus Power Assembly is not just a church; it is a spiritual home where lives are refined and destinies are birthed. We believe in the raw power of the Holy Spirit to transform the most ordinary life into an extraordinary testimony.

Whether you're joining us for the first time or looking for a place to grow in faith, there's a space for you here.

"At Jesus Power Assembly, we believe in the transforming power of Jesus Christ."
— Archbishop David Jude Inegbenehi

Our Heart & Mission

Our History

Founded on a mandate of power, the ministry has grown from a mustard seed into a global phenomenon, planting branches and touching thousands of lives through prophetic fire.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of power-filled believers who dominate their environments through the knowledge of God's Word and the exercise of apostolic authority.

Core Values

Faith, Power, Love, Transformation, Community. These are the pillars that uphold every department and ministry within our assembly.

Our Spiritual Leadership

Founder & Senior Pastor

Archbishop David Jude Inegbenehi

Archbishop David Jude Inegbenehi is a man of profound spiritual insight and apostolic grace. With over three decades in ministry, he has become a leading voice in the charismatic movement, known for his unwavering commitment to the Word and the miraculous.

  • Founder of Jesus Power Assembly Int'l
  • Global Evangelist and Prophetic Voice
  • Author of several life-transforming books
  • Spiritual father to hundreds of ministries
Co-Pastor & First Lady

Reverend Mrs. Inegbenehi

Reverend Mrs. Inegbenehi serves alongside her husband with grace, wisdom, and a mother's heart. She is the pillar of the women's ministry and a devoted partner in the work of the Lord, especially gifted in counseling and spiritual nurture.

A Legacy of Faith

Archbishop & Reverend Mrs. Inegbenehi are blessed with four children, all of whom are ordained pastors wholly consecrated to the service of God.

Pastor Priscillia Inegbenehi

Pastor Priscillia serves faithfully in the body of Christ, carrying the grace and anointing of her father's ministry with elegance and devotion.

Resident Pastor – Headquarters

Pastor Ehinomen Inegbenehi

Pastor Ehinomen faithfully oversees the headquarters church, shepherding the flock with wisdom and the fire of the Holy Spirit in the Archbishop's absence.

Resident Pastor – Branch

Pastor Prince Inegbenehi

Pastor Prince leads the Medical Store Room Road branch with zeal and apostolic fire, extending the power of Jesus Christ in Benin City.

Pastor Praise Inegbenehi

Pastor Praise serves in the ministry with a heart of worship and intercession, carrying a unique anointing that draws people into God's presence.

✨ A Double Portion – Blessed with Twins in Ministry: Pastor Prince & Pastor Praise Inegbenehi
